From Ashes to Glory: The PHANTOM VI Story

PHANTOM VI was established in 2015 by Vincent Makafu in the Free State. Starting from humble beginnings, Vincent’s vision and relentless dedication turned PHANTOM VI into a respected name in the liquor industry.

Growing from those early days, Vincent has built a legacy of excellence and trust, now operating successfully out of Colesberg. His commitment to quality and innovation makes him one of the best in the game.

PHANTOM VI is more than just a brand; it’s a story of resilience, genius, and passion — rising from ashes to glory.

Licensing & Legal Reality in South Africa

Navigating South Africa's liquor licensing landscape can be challenging. It’s crucial not to rush the process. PHANTOM VI offers expert advice to help you understand the regulations and requirements.

In the interim, you can sell your product under PHANTOM VI's existing license, allowing you to showcase your brand at events, venues, or as corporate gifts without the immediate burden of obtaining your own license.

When You're Ready to License

PHANTOM VI will guide you through the licensing process when you're ready.

  • Main Licensing Bodies: The provincial liquor boards.
  • Costs: Vary by province and license type; includes application, inspection, and renewal fees.
  • Process: Application submission, inspections, hearings.
  • Investment & Timeline: Costs range from R5,000 to R50,000+, timeline from 3 to 12 months or more.
