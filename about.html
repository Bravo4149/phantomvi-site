From Ashes to Glory: Our Story

PHANTOM VI was established by Vincent Makafu in 2015, born in the heart of the Free State, South Africa. With nothing but a vision, he began crafting high-quality spirits that echoed elegance, authenticity, and local pride.

What started as a small operation has now grown into a respected custom alcohol brand operating proudly from Colesberg. Vincent is regarded as one of the most determined and innovative figures in the local beverage scene—proof that resilience, creativity, and vision can lead from ashes to glory.

Branding & Licensing Support

We help you brand your own bottles with label design, logo refinement, and strategy guidance. For newcomers to the industry, we allow promotional sales under the PHANTOM VI license—compliant with South African liquor laws.
