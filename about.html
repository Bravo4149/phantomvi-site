From Ashes to Glory

PHANTOM VI was founded in 2015 by Vincent Makafu in the Free State. What started as a dream with almost nothing has grown into one of South Africa’s most unique custom alcohol brands. Through years of perseverance, passion, and perfection, Vincent now operates PHANTOM VI from Colesberg, leading the game in custom wines, gins, and vodkas.

Today, PHANTOM VI is known for helping others build their own liquor brands, print custom labels, and make unforgettable impressions with personalized bottles.

Licensing & Legal Support

South Africa’s liquor licensing process is complex. That’s why PHANTOM VI offers guidance to help you navigate it without rushing into legal traps.

While you're still getting started, you can promote and sell your product under our existing license for demo purposes — including at events, venues, and as corporate gifts.

Please Note: This support is for promotional use only and must comply with all South African liquor laws.

When You're Ready to License

  • Main Bodies: Provincial Liquor Boards
  • Costs: R5,000–R50,000+ depending on license type and location
  • Time: Typically 3–12+ months
  • Includes: Application forms, site inspections, public hearings, renewals
