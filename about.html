From Ashes to Glory

PHANTOM VI was founded in 2015 by Vincent Makafu in the Free State with a vision to create a unique and memorable drink experience. Starting from humble beginnings, Vincent refined his craft, poured heart into branding, and built lasting relationships with distilleries and bottle decorators across the country.

After years of perseverance and passion, PHANTOM VI now proudly operates in Colesberg, offering custom wine, gin, and vodka bottles. Vincent is recognized as a pioneer in personalized liquor branding — “the best in the game.”

Licensing & Legal Reality in South Africa

Navigating South Africa's liquor laws can be overwhelming. At PHANTOM VI, we understand the landscape and help guide you through it. While you're building your brand, we allow partners to trade under our existing license for promotions, events, and gifting. This is fully compliant when handled responsibly and with transparency.

Note: This is for promotional and demonstration purposes only and must strictly adhere to South African liquor laws.

When You're Ready to License

Once your brand is ready to operate independently, PHANTOM VI will help you secure your own liquor license.

  • Main Licensing Bodies: Provincial liquor boards
  • Costs: Range from R5,000 to R50,000+ depending on your area and needs
  • Process: Application, inspections, hearings
  • Timeline: 3 to 12 months or longer
