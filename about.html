About Phantom VI

PHANTOM VI was founded in 2015 by Vincent Makafu in the heart of the Free State. What started as a humble vision turned into one of South Africa’s most promising independent liquor brands.

Now based in Colesberg, Vincent is considered one of the best in the custom wine and gin game. With hard work and unmatched vision, he grew PHANTOM VI into a premium solution for individuals looking to start or elevate their own alcohol brands.

We offer full product development, brand consultation, and licensing guidance even allowing new businesses to operate under our license for demos and events. Your brand deserves a story. Let us help you tell it.
