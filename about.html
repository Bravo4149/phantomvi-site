Our Story

PHANTOM VI was founded in 2015 by Vincent Makafu in the Free State. With grit, creativity, and passion, he built the business from humble beginnings, eventually expanding operations to Colesberg.

Today, Phantom VI is a respected name in custom alcohol bottling, label printing, and private-brand support, known for transforming dreams into real, tangible products. From ashes to glory — Vincent’s journey is a true tale of resilience and excellence in the industry.

Licensing & Legal in South Africa

Licensing in SA can be complex, Phantom VI is here to help. You can sell under our existing license for promotions, events, and demos.

Note: All sales must comply with South African liquor laws.

  • Main Bodies: Provincial liquor boards
  • Costs: R5,000 – R50,000+ depending on type and province
  • Process: Application → Inspections → Hearing
  • Timeline: 3 to 12 months

When you’re ready for your own license, we’ll guide you every step of the way.
