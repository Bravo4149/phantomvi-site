From Ashes to Glory

PHANTOM VI was established in 2015 by Vincent Makafu in the Free State province of South Africa. Born with a burning passion for branding, Vincent started with humble beginnings, selling only a few bottles with hand-made labels. Over the years, through perseverance and pure genius, he grew PHANTOM VI into one of the most respected names in custom-labelled liquor production.

Today, PHANTOM VI operates out of Colesberg, offering premium-quality wine, gin, and vodka, uniquely crafted and custom-labeled for events, businesses, and personal brands. With a commitment to taste, aesthetics, and experience — we transform ordinary bottles into unforgettable masterpieces.

Branding Support

Your brand's identity is crucial. We offer comprehensive branding support to ensure your product stands out. Our services include label design, logo refinement, and overall brand strategy. Whether you’re launching your first product or enhancing an existing one, we’ve got you covered.

Licensing & Legal Reality in South Africa

Navigating South Africa's liquor licensing landscape can be challenging. It's important not to rush into the licensing process without expert guidance. PHANTOM VI provides advisory support to help you understand the legal requirements and licensing options.

In the interim, you can sell your product under PHANTOM VI’s existing license — perfect for events, tastings, gifts, and promotional showcases. This allows you to start building your brand without delay.

Please Note: All operations must adhere to South African liquor laws. This option is for promotional purposes only.

When You're Ready to License

Once your brand is ready to operate independently, PHANTOM VI will guide you through obtaining your own liquor license.

  • Main Licensing Bodies: Provincial liquor boards
  • Costs: R 5,000 to R 50,000+ depending on province and type of license
  • Process: Includes application, inspections, and hearings
  • Timeline: 3 to 12+ months depending on local conditions
